19 years old and Born and raised in Baltimore Maryland

It's just me, my little corner of the world (which also happens to be the internet at this point). If for some reason, you came across this website not knowing who I am, my name is Ethan Joyner. Born and raised in Baltimore, I am currently 19, and I am going into my college sophomore year. I attend the Community College of Baltimore County, and my major is computer engineering.

A funny thing, making a website is tedious, but creating content is the most challenging part of the site, because it's a portrayal of myself. I am grounded in who I am, but it's weird when social media constantly makes you consolidate your thoughts and ideas into a single paragraph, but now I have an entire platform to express and broadcast myself. Where to start?

As a kid, the idea of building something has always intrigued me. So I joined the Lego club in 4th grade. I made whatever I wanted and loved it. As time went on, I was introduced to Lego Mindstorms, a creative robotics kit for early exposure to engineering. We all found our strong suits with building robots, and we carried our skills to high school. But of course, similar to my earlier school years, I found a way to surround myself in similar creative minds as we sketched ideas, programmed, and built robots.

I was fortunate enough to have plenty of lucky breaks in my life, which is a privilege amongst itself. From writing guess code to getting access to software and hardware that I never thought I could own. Though I can't share any of those things, the least I can do is share my knowledge and insight as to how I go about my design process. Though I am still learning my self, I may have the advantage in making and sharing the real rookie mistakes I make in my projects, sharing the how-not-to's and, of course, the alternatives.
